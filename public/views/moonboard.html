Context

I created this project with Audrey Chow, as a exercise to improve in interaction design. Our project focuses on aligning and opening up opportunities for elite climbers to access the board. This interaction design project was completed as a four week sprint, with an additional two weeks to refine and polish the prototype.

What is a Moonboard?

Each wall is set up with identical LED holds and connects to a mobile app, which gives users the ability to browse problems, log their completed climbs, and compare their performance against friends and professional athletes. All of the problems are set by users on the app, and reviewed by other climbers for a mutually agreed difficulty level.

From amateur to world class, climbers use the Moonboard to train their strength and power endurance.

Project Role

Interaction Design, User Research, Prototyping
I co-led the interaction design, and brought to life the interaction with a mix of prototyping tools, After Effects, Premiere Pro, and Audacity. User Research was conducted by the whole team, but I specifically focused on researching and understanding the importance of informal conversation. I pulled key insights from both interviews and the literature review that ended up informing the final design decisions.
