About

I’m Ethan, an digital designer and web developer based in Vancouver BC. I specialize in designing in spaces that allow for emerging technology, to establish new interaction design patterns. In doing so, I ultimately strive to create sustainable solutions designed for the future. When not stressing over pixels or pulling my hair out from coding, you can find me on a local BC mountain, either climbing or cycling.

I am currently a senior at Simon Fraser University, studying at the School of Interactive Arts and Technology with a concentration in design.
